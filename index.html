Efi's version of Rock, Paper, Scissors



Rules of the game

  1. Rock ✊ "crushes" Scissors ✂️
  2. Paper 🧻 "covers" Rock ✊
  3. Scissors ✂️ "cuts" Paper 🧻
  4. For every win, the winner gets one(1) point
  5. No marks are awarded for draws
  6. The first to earn 10 points wins the game
